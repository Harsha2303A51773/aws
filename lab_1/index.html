About the Symposium

The National Technical Symposium brings together students, researchers and industry experts from across India to share research, innovations, and practical solutions. We host keynote talks, paper presentations, workshops and project demos.

  • Keynotes from industry leaders
  • Paper presentations & workshops
  • Student project expo & hackathon
